Brief

Illumination = Emissive+Ambient+Diffuse+Specular+Refraction

  • Flat
    • Whole surface has same color;
  • Gouraud
    • Calculate color in all surface vertex, and lerp all other color;
  • Phong
    • Lerp normal for all surface pixel and calculate color for all pixel.

Emissive

I = Ie

Ambient

I = Ka*Ia

Ka depends on surface

Ia is ambient light

Diffuse

I = Kd*Il*(N.L)

Kd depends on surface

Il is diffuse light

N is surface normal direction

L is lighting direction

if N.L<0 then ignore it and treat as black(no light).
